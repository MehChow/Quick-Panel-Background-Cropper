# What's New in v3

Quick Panel Background Cropper v3 adds a complete way to create backgrounds for
selected Quick Panel Buttons, while keeping the existing Controls workflows.

## Customize Quick Panel Buttons

Choose **Advanced**, then **Buttons only** to:

- select one or more Buttons from a searchable list of 30 common Samsung
  Buttons;
- add your own custom Button label and choose an icon for it;
- match Buttons of different sizes and shapes to your screenshot;
- place one image continuously across all selected Buttons; and
- export every Button as a separate PNG in the order shown by the app.

Button selection is manual because Android does not give normal apps access to
your complete Samsung Quick Settings Button list.

## More control over the final look

The Buttons preview now includes controls for:

- background image intensity;
- showing or hiding Button identifiers;
- identifier intensity; and
- identifier position on long horizontal or vertical Buttons.

Identifiers use a white icon and, when the Button shape allows it, its label.
The app adapts their layout to square, horizontal, vertical, and larger custom
Button shapes. Your Buttons appearance settings are remembered for the next
time you use the app.

## Improved Advanced calibration

- Advanced now has separate **Controls only** and **Buttons only** targets.
- Each target keeps its own saved calibration.
- You can preview the screenshot area you selected before aligning panels.
- The snapping grid can be turned off for free movement and resizing, and the
  choice is remembered separately for Controls and Buttons.
- The app remembers your last successfully exported mode and Advanced target.

## Smoother preview and export

Calibration, image movement, and multi-Button export have been optimized for a
smoother experience. The live Buttons preview and exported PNGs now share the
same image placement and identifier layout, helping the final QuickStar result
match what you arranged in the app.

Built-in Button labels are available in English and Traditional Chinese.
Custom labels stay exactly as you enter them.

## Important update note

After updating from the current Google Play v1.0.0 release, you will need to
calibrate your layout once again. This is required because v3 uses corrected
calibration coordinates so the visible green selection matches the exported
crop accurately.

Your unrelated preferences are not cleared. After the new calibration is
saved, Default, Advanced Controls, and Advanced Buttons layouts are stored
separately for future use.

## What stays the same

- Default mode remains for the four supported Controls panels.
- Advanced Controls continues to support Button box, Brightness, Volume, and
  Media player.
- v3 does not combine Controls and Buttons in the same export run.
- Exported PNGs are still applied manually through Samsung Good Lock QuickStar.
